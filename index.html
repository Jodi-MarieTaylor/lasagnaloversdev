Who We Are

We are a home-based catering company that prides itself in making delicious lasagnas with the freshest ingredients. Friends and family members have always adored our lasagna, so we decided to take the plunge and open our doors in May 2017. We’ve been sharing our creation with Jamaica ever since. We cater to meat lovers, seafood addicts, vegetarians and everyone in between. When placing orders please let us know about any allergies you may have so that we may handle with the utmost care.
